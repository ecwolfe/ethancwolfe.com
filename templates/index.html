About me

I am a recent graduate from Auburn University with a degree in Computer Science. I chose Computer Science to gain access to a broad field filled with interesting problems, and my education has provided me with a strong grasp on the fundamental principals of programming. I personally have enjoyed working on projects that illuminate important abstractions, processes, and patterns while also solving immediately useful and interesting problems. For example, A Boggle Solver using a recursive algorithm to find all walks on a game board, that also is testing each walk for possible solutions, or Markov Monkey a random text generator that uses Markov chains to build psuedo random text. I also enjoy working on personal projects, such as this website which I made to display some of my old projects and to learn all the basic skills required to build and host my own website.

Outside of work I enjoy many means of passing time. Running is an important part of my everyday routine, as it helps me to stay healthy and focused. I have recently started learning German online using Duolingo it provides an easy path of lessons and encourages daily use for better retension. I enjoy reading A variety of books from programming, science fiction, to philosophy.
